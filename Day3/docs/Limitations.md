# Limitations

* The agent does not have direct access to live employee records unless integrated with HRMS databases.
* Leave balances, payroll details, and attendance information cannot be verified in real time without backend connectivity.
* Responses are based on configured policies and prompts.
* The agent may require human HR intervention for exceptions and complex employee cases.
* Policy updates must be manually reflected in the knowledge base.
* The agent cannot make HR decisions or approvals.
* Personal employee information is protected and cannot be disclosed without authorization.
